Canyon Whisper

A serene journey designed for those seeking harmony with nature. Glide through mirrored waters surrounded by ancient cliffs. Perfect for families with children and landscape photography enthusiasts.

Dragon's Dance

Feel the pulse of the river on our most popular mid-level run. Navigate rhythmic cascades and learn to work as a team through white-water sprays. A high-energy adventure for thrill-seekers.

Atlas Fury

The ultimate test of skill and courage. This expedition tackles technical Class V rapids in the narrowest parts of the gorge. Reserved strictly for experienced rafters ready to face the river's full power.
